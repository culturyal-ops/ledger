## 🎨 Design Philosophy

### Luxury Principles Applied:
1. **Generous Letter-Spacing**: 0.5px - 1px on small text (buttons, headers, captions) - "feels expensive"
2. **Pill-Shaped Buttons**: 40px border-radius for modern luxury feel
3. **UPPERCASE Text**: On buttons, badges, card titles, table headers - premium branding
4. **Multi-Layer Shadows**: Not simple rgba(0,0,0,0.1) - layered for depth
5. **Gold Emphasis**: First column in tables is bold/semi-bold for hierarchy
6. **4-Stop Gradients**: Complex gold-shine gradient for metallic effect
7. **Warm Backgrounds**: Cream (#FDF9F3) not stark white - reduces eye strain
8. **Thin Scrollbar**: 8px width - elegant and refined
9. **Micro-Animations**: 0.2s - 0.3s smooth transitions throughout
10. **Typography Hierarchy**: Logo 26px/-0.5px, Titles 32px/-0.3px, proper scale
